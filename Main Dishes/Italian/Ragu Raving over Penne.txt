Ragu Raving over Penne

Ingredients
	4 slices lean bacon
	1 pound ground turkey
	1 cup sliced button mushrooms
	1 large sweet onion, diced
	4-6 garlic cloves, minced
	1 bell pepper, diced
	1 celery rib, diced (1/2 cup)
	4 Tablespoons mixed Italian herbs
	1 pinch red pepper flakes
	1/2 cup red wine (optional)
	20 ounces Rotel tomatoes, pureed
	20 ounces tomato sauce
	1 Tablespoon brown sugar
	kosher salt
	fresh cracked black pepper
	freshly grated parmesan cheese, to taste
	1-2 Tablespoon butter
	4 fresh basil leaves
	1 pound penne pasta, cooked according to directions
	freshly grated parmesan cheese, to taste
	red pepper flakes, to taste
	
Method
	1. Cook bacon till crisp, draining and discarding grease. 
		Crumble and place in crockpot with remaining ingredients except for
		salt, pepper, pasta, cheese, butter and basil.
	2. Mix everything together so ingredients are all incorporated.
	3. Cook on low for 4-6 hours. Stir when you can.
	4. Before serving adjust seasoning with salt and pepper and add additional Italian
		seasonings to your taste to refresh the herbiness. Add butter and fresh basil and
		mix in for extra richness and freshness.
	5. Plate pasta and pour sauce over it then top with cheese and pepper flakes.
