Mama's Lasagna

Yields 6 to 8 servings

Ingredients
	1 Tablespoon olive oil
	1 pound ground beef or turkey
	1 medium onion, chopped
	1 clove garlic, minced
	1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, chopped
	1 (8 ounce) jar tomato sauce
	1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
	1 (8 ounce) box no-boil lasagna noodles
	2 large eggs
	2 cups cottage cheese
	1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
	2 teaspoons freshly chopped parsley leaves
	1 teaspoon salt
	1 teaspoon black pepper
	1 teaspoon seasoning salt
	1 (8 ounce) bag shredded mozzarella
	1 (8 ounce) bay shredded Cheddar
	
Method
	1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F and lightly oil the bottom of a 13x9x2 inch baking dish.
	2. In a large saute pan, over medium-high heat, add oil and saute meat, onion, 
		and garlic until meat is browned, breaking it up with a wooden spoon.
	3. Drain pan of fat and add stewed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste.
	4. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
	5. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, then mix in cottage cheese, 
		1/2 cup Parmesan, parsley, salt, pepper, and seasoning salt.
	6. Spread a little of the meat sauce in the bottom of the prepared pan.
	7. Lay half the noodles in the bottom of the baking dish, overlapping by 1/2 inch.
	8. Spread half the egg and cottage cheese mixture evenly on top.
	9. Sprinkle half the mozzarella and Cheddar evenly over cottage cheese mixture.
	10. Pour half the meat sauce on top.
	11. Repeat layering in the same order.
	12. Sprinkle remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan on top.
	13. Bake in center of oven 30 to 35 minutes until sauce is bubbling around the edges.
	14. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
