Parmesan Round Steak

Ingredients
	1 1/2 to 2 pounds round steak
	1 egg, beaten 
	1/3 cup milk
	1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs
	1 teaspoon salt
	1/8 teaspoon pepper
	1/2 teaspoon paprika
	3 Tablespoons bacon drippings or 1 Tablespoon of butter and 2 Tablespoons vegetable oil
	1/2 cup water
	1/4 teaspoon dried leaf oregano
	1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
	1/4 teaspoon salt
	1/4 teaspoon paprika
	6 small onions, peeled and sliced
	
Method
	1. Cut steak into 6 serving size pieces and pound to 1/2-inch thickness.
	2. Combine egg and milk.
	3. Mix bread crumbs, salt, pepper and paprika.
	4. Dip steaks in egg mixture; dredge in seasoned crumbs.
	5. In a large skillet, brown meat in bacon drippings. If skillet is not oven proof, 
		move steaks to a baking pan.
	6. Add water to skillet. (If using baking pan, add water to skillet and scrape 
		browned bits before pouring water over steaks).
	7. Sprinkle oregano on steaks. Place 2 teaspoons Parmesan cheese on each steak.
	8. Combine remaining salt and paprika. 
	9. Sprinkle onions with paprika mixture and add to steaks.
	10. Cover skillet or baking pan tightly with foil or lid and cook at 325Â° for 
		1 1/4 to 1 1/2 hours or until meat is tender.
