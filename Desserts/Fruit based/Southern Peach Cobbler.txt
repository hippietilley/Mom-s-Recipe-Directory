Southern Peach Cobbler

Ingredients
	For the cobbler
		1/2 cup (1 stick) of butter
		1 cup of sugar
		3/4 cup self-rising flour 
		3/4 cup of milk
	
	For the fruit
		1 can (28 ounce) peaches in heavy syrup
		or 
		2 cups fresh sliced peaches
		1 cup of sugar
		1 cup of water
		
	1 Tablespoon cinnamon (for sprinkling on top)
	
Method
	1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. 
	2. Put the butter in a 13x9x2 inch baking dish and place in the oven until butter is melted.
		Remove from oven and set aside.
	3. In a bowl, mix together the flour and sugar and stir in the milk. Batter wil have a 
		few lumps but that's okay.
	4. Pour the batter on top of the melted butter.
	5. If using fresh peaches, place in a saucepan with the sugar and water and bring to a boil.
		Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
	6. Spoon fruit on top of batter and then slowly pour the liquid on top of that.
		Be careful not to mix the fruit into the batter. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
	7. Bake for 35 to 45 minutes. Batter will rise to the top.
